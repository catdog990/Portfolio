Whats Up with Meh

What makes a great developer? This is a question I ask myself everytime I'm working on a new project. Creativity, patience, and work ethic would probably be some of the things you think I would say. Now while theses qualities are important, they are not what make a good developer in my opinion. Constantly learning and having the stones to follow through on your ideas are far more important. These qualities are what I bring to my projects; I am not afraid to take on projects that involve learning, or push broundaries.
